A little about myself

I'm a young 20 year old developer from Charlotte, NC who's been teaching himself for about 2 years. In early 2016 I attended Tech Talent South's Code Immersion Bootcamp to gain a solid foundation and meet some amazing people. Half way through the course I lost my internet connection for about a month, so what do you do when you don't have the internet? You create the internet! Through this journey I've been introduced to things I've never imagined were in this city, and I'm always hungry to know more. Seeing as I'm still young and passionate about what I do, this is the perfect time to get into the industry.
